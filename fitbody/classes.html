Premium Fitness Classes

Cycling
This course meets you where you are at, everyone is welcome. We will have 60 minute guided cycling sessions, where an instructor will lead you to cycle at your relative level. Come ride with us and get your blood pumping! A great way to get aerobic excercise!
Crossfit
This is a rigorous course, not for the faint of heart. Prepare yourself for 60 minute high intensity workouts targeted at working all the parts of your body — legs, arms, chest, back, core, and cardio building both your strength and your endurance to peak levels.
Aqua Aerobics
For a gentle and low-impact course, look no further! In our swimming pools we have 40 minute guided water aerobics courses intended to gently engage in resistence training to improve blood flow, and build muscle in a low impact way to protect your joints and body.
